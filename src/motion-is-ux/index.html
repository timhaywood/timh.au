Motion Design is UX

There's a few things that stood out to me when transitioning into UI motion design, one of them being that it's really UX role.

As an animator in other areas, your goal is to make great looking motion that serves the story.

In the world of UI, your goal is to create a great user experience, using motion.

This is done, in part, by transitioning between states in a way that makes things clear to the user.

Most of the work is in understanding the relationship between these states, so you can animate transitions that help clarify these relationships.

It's important to do this with a user focused perspective. Rather than focusing on the motion itself, focusing on how the motion affects the users experience of the software.
